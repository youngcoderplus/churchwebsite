Children & Youth

        St. Mary’s has a vibrant children and youth ministry.

Our Sunday school program welcomes children of all ages and abilities. Bible stories, games and crafts keep everybody busy.  Babies, toddlers and moms are welcome to join us, or you can enjoy the service with your children together on the Cozy Corner couches.  We've got something that works for everyone.  
Messy Church is the first Friday of the month at 5:30 pm from October to June.

With Gratitude

The Diocese of British Columbia acknowledges that for thousands of years the Coast Salish, Nuu-chah-nulth, and Kwakwaka’wakw peoples have walked gently on the unceded territories where we now live, work, worship, and play. We seek a new relationship with the first peoples here, one based in honour and respect, and we thank them for their hospitality.
